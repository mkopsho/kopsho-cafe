Oh hey, these are some of my code projects I've been tinkering on.

📚 Homebrew Ledger

A small web app to help store and track homebrew recipes. Has a large database with all commercially available ingredients.

If you are someone who likes to homebrew beer and would like a convenient place to store, edit, and track your recipes, this tool is for you. It can also be used by others to store and share their recipes, too!

Ingredients: Ruby, Sinatra, Bulma

⚔️ DND Companion

A helper app for fledgling Dungeon Masters that displays spells, conditions, and monsters in Dungeons and Dragons.

Ingredients: Ruby, DnD5e API
